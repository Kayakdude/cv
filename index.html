Meet Our Team

Our trainers will take your training to a new level through support and knowledge


Sid

Sid Cumming.

Gym Owner, Cycling NZ Coach.


Sid and Sharalyn have been owners in the gym industry for 30 years. You will usually find Sid riding a bike in the mornings, chatting with members, taking a spin class at night or maintaining the gym equipment. When Sid isn’t in the gym, he is working hard coaching for Cycling NZ.

Zane

Zane Harris.

Operations Manager/Massage Therapist.


Zane is our Jack of all trades. His main role is to look after the staff and ensure the gym is running smoothly. You might also find him training clients or maintaining equipment. Zane has also been a part of the World Health and Fitness crew for 15 years as the owner of Sportz Plus – our on-site Massage Therapy Clinic.

Sharyn

Sharyn.

Gym Owner, Administrator.


Sharyn and Doug have been owners in the gym industry for 30 years. You will usually find Sharyn popping in and out of the gym, ensuring the premises are clean and tidy for the members. Sharyn is also the other half of the admin team with her daughter Bex.

Bex

Bex Andrews.

Admin Assistant/Beauty Therapist.


Being Doug and Sharyn’s daughter, Bex has been a part of the World Health and Fitness family for almost 30 years. Bex main role is to work hard behind the scenes looking after all the accounts and membership payments. Bex also owns Wicked Pretty – our on-site Beauty Therapy clinic.
